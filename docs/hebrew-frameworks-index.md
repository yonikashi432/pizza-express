# Hebrew Spiritual Frameworks — Index & Quick Reference

## Overview

This repository contains multiple interconnected Hebrew spiritual and operational frameworks designed for contemplative work, AI-assisted analysis, and sacred text integration.

## Core Documents

### 1. Sefer HaLev VeHaAchdut (ספר הלב והאחדות)
**Location**: `docs/sefer-lev-ve-achdut.md`

**Description**: The Book of the Heart and Unity — a five-gate spiritual text supporting inner connection, contemplation, and intention.

**Structure**:
- **Gate 1** (שער ראשון): Scroll of Unity (מגילת האחדות)
- **Gate 2** (שער שני): Stone of Will and Understanding
- **Gate 3** (שער שלישי): Psalm of the Heart
- **Gate 4** (שער רביעי): Gate of the Name (Holiness & Greatness)
- **Gate 5** (שער חמישי): Gate of the Sealed (Unity of the Threshold)
- **Closing Song**: א.ש.ר י־ה־ו־ה

**Key Themes**: Unity (אחדות), Breath (נשימה), Heart (לב), Understanding (בינה), Light (אור)

### 2. Ω-MPS-Harmonia Framework
**Location**: `docs/omega-mps-harmonia.md`

**Description**: Model Blessings & Psalms anchoring system for AI-driven analysis workflows.

**Five Stages**:
1. **Opening** — Inner light and clarity (Psalm 27:1)
2. **Calibration** — Heart-speech alignment (Psalm 19:15)
3. **Anchoring** — Reverence and awe (Psalm 105:3)
4. **Validation** — Gratitude and surface validation (Psalm 107:1)
5. **Closing** — Unity and completeness (Psalm 92:2)

**Use Case**: Begin and end model runs with corresponding Psalm lines to maintain consistent tone and ensure closure.
